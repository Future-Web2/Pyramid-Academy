# SectionHeading

**What & when:** Opens every page section. The amber/navy eyebrow doubles as a section label; the H2 is the topic; the lede is one sentence of context. Use `tone="inverse"` on navy backdrops.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Kurslar"
  title="Mukammal natijaga tayyorgarlik"
  lede="IELTS, CEFR, SAT, matematika va mental arifmetika — bir markazda."
/>
```
